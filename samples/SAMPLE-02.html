About Chizel

Our mission to make learning fun and effective for every child

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of educators and game developers, Chizel was born out of a simple idea: what if learning could be as engaging as the games kids love to play? We set out to create an educational platform that would harness the power of play to help children develop essential skills while having fun.

Today, Chizel is used by over 500,000 families worldwide, helping children build confidence in math, reading, and problem-solving through our unique game-based approach.

Our Approach

At Chizel, we believe that learning should be:

  • Engaging: Games that kids want to play, not have to play
  • Effective: Research-backed methods that actually teach
  • Empowering: Builds confidence through gradual challenges
  • Transparent: Clear progress tracking for parents
